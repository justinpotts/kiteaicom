Abuse and harassment detection

Solve the #1 problem on the internet today, with Kite AI's abuse detection. Kite AI is 90% accurate, and continues to climb higher as we add to our millions of existing datapoints. Abuse detection is the first step in solving harassment in your app.

Topic Modeling

We know some things can be considered abusive towards some, and others not so much. Topic modeling provides you with the subject and context of the conversation. This is great for forum moderation, or even tailoring content towards certain user profiles. Additionally, this is useful for apps like parental control software where you can analyze the type of content your child may be viewing online.

Frequency and Severity

Some platforms are different than others. Maybe your users have a threshold for the content they like to see, or maybe you want to set a threshold for what's considered acceptable. With Kite AI, we'll provide you with the frequency/severity for each topic, whether it's nonexistant, low, medium, or high.

Sentiment Analysis

Tone can say a lot. In addition to whether or not a comment is considered abuse or harassment, we'll tell you the sentiment of the phrase. Use this to determine the overall mood of the conversation, or track conversation flow between messages.

Developer Dashboard

With the developer dashboard, we make it easy for you to create apps, manage billing, and monitor usage. You'll be given access to the dashboard upon signup, and it's complete free.

Viewing an exporting your data

Using the developer dashboard, you can keep track of the kinds of requests coming through your applicaiton. View usage statistic per application, and export data for use in your projects.

Entity Analysis (coming soon)

Similar to topic modeling, we want Kite AI to be customizable to your needs. Entity analysis will give you a detailed breakdown of certain user profiles this comment may offend, and certain user profiles who may consider this comment acceptable. Use entity analysis to custom tailor your platform to your users.
